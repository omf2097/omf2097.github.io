Binaries

Our latest official releases are always available at https://github.com/omf2097/openomf/releases. The official releases are always tested to work, but may be heavily out of date.

There are unofficial windows and linux binaries available at https://buildbot.openomf.org/artifacts/. These are updated every now and then, and may or may not work. Use with caution!

Sources

The sources are available at our main repository at http://www.github.com/omf2097/openomf/ and as downloadable archives (master branch) from the links below:

  • Download source as a .tar.gz file
